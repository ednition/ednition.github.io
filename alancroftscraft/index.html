About Me

About Me

From RAF boy entrant to devoted husband, Christian artist and charity volunteer

  • Name: Alan Croft
  • Age: 78
  • From: Ripon, U.K.
  • Lives in: Harrogate, U.K.
  • Occupation: Retired
  • Former Career: 14 years in the RAF boy entrants
  • Volunteer: Starbeck Men's Shed
  • Email: allan.croft@outlook.com

Artist Woodwork, Acrylic Paintings, Watercolouring and Drawings.

Royal Air Force Alan entered the RAF via the boy entrants scheme. He served for 14 years visiting countries such as Cyprus, Bahrain, Nairobi, Mali and others.

Starbeck Men's Shed Treasurer and volunteer. The aim of the Starbeck based Shed is to provide a place for like-minded men to come together and work on a project, learn a new skill or use an old one and get out of the house. The Shed is open to all men over 50 living in North Yorkshire.
